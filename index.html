Your Questions, Answered

Is this truly free? What's the catch?

Yes — 100% free. Empowered is Trainocate's initiative to make quality AI education accessible across Southeast Asia. No credit card, no trial period, no hidden fees. Register once and gain immediate, unlimited access to all course content.

Do I need a technical background to start?

Not at all. Most courses are designed for non-technical professionals — business leaders, managers, knowledge workers, and everyday Microsoft 365 users. The AI Engineers track does assume some technical familiarity, but all other courses start from the ground up.

Which course should I start with?

If you use Microsoft 365 daily, start with "Mastering AI & Microsoft 365" — it's the most comprehensive and practical. If you're in a leadership or strategy role, "AI for Business Leaders" is the sharper fit. If you write a lot of prompts or want to get better at working with AI tools, go straight to "Prompt Engineering for Executives & End Users."

How much time will it take to complete all courses?

The full AI Skills library totals approximately 8.4 hours of video content. Because it's fully on-demand, most learners complete it in one to two weeks by watching a couple of modules each day. You can also pick just the courses most relevant to your role and skip the rest.

Who is this platform designed for?

Empowered is built for working professionals at every level — from executives and team leads to individual contributors and engineers. If you want to build real, applicable AI skills without a lengthy, costly training commitment, this is for you.
